〉PRODUCT CATALOG [1/9]

All the legos to build the perfect context for your agent.

Focused primitives for ingesting, understanding, routing, and retrieving context.

01 · MEMORY

Memory & Continual Learning

State-of-the-art memory that is persistent, structured, and built as a knowledge graph using our custom user understanding model, powered by dynamic dreaming and a custom graph engine.

02 · RETRIEVAL

SuperRAG (Retrieval)

Hybrid search, reranking, and structured context for documents at sub-300ms latency. The retrieval layer your agent reaches for when it needs more than what's already in working memory.

03 · FILESYSTEMS

Filesystems

A real filesystem mount that gives your agent a supermemory. grep becomes semantic search, a live profile.md synthesizes context on the fly, and any file format is indexed automatically. Native POSIX on macOS and Linux; your agent just uses ls, cat, and grep.

04 · PROFILES

Profiles

Preference, behavior, and identity context that stays coherent across sessions. Your agent remembers the person on the other side of the screen, not just the conversation in front of it.

05 · CONNECTORS

Connectors

Connect Slack, Notion, Drive, Gmail, GitHub, S3, and custom sources; Supermemory automatically keeps them in sync. Changes in your tools are reflected in your agent's memory in real time. No manual imports, no stale context, no ETL.

06 · PARSING

Extractors

Turn PDFs, web pages, images, audio, and raw files into agent-ready memory objects. Smart chunking that preserves meaning across document boundaries, not arbitrary character counts. No need to sign up for 10 different providers; Supermemory handles the extraction for you.

07 · UNDERSTANDING

Qualitative Analysis

Cluster, summarize, and explain user signals without exporting the dataset. The understanding layer runs against your memory in place; your data never leaves the boundary you set.

〉FAQ [9/9]
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

The fine print, in plain English.

  1. Every plan comes with a monthly dollar balance. Each API call, storing memories, searching, indexing, draws from that balance at the rates listed above.

    When the balance runs out, you can top up or auto top-up to keep going. No surprise bills at the end of the month.

  2. SM tokens are the unique tokens Supermemory actually ingests and embeds; repeats and unchanged content don't get billed again. Effectively a 100% discount on what a normal prompt cache would re-charge you for.

    Plain text is $0.005 per 1K SM tokens; rich content (PDFs, audio, video) is $0.010 per 1K because it needs heavier extraction.

    PLAIN TEXT $0.005 / 1K
    RICH CONTENT $0.010 / 1K

  3. Nothing, you're not billed again. Supermemory deduplicates at the token level, so re-uploading a doc, syncing a connector, or pushing the same conversation history won't redraw from your balance.

    Only net-new content counts as SM tokens. This is why production agents that loop over the same context end up an order of magnitude cheaper here than with a typical vector DB.

  4. Subscription credits reset monthly. Top-up credits you purchase in advance never expire, they sit in your balance until you use them.

  5. On Free, you'll be paused; pay-as-you-go isn't available on Free, so you'll need to upgrade to Pro or Scale to keep going.

    On Pro and Scale, auto top-up kicks in to keep your app running. You can set hard spend caps on Scale to prevent runaway usage.

  6. Self-hosted deployments are available on Scale and Enterprise. Enterprise additionally supports fully air-gapped deployments (LLM inference may be the only outbound dependency, unless GPUs are available).

  7. Yes. Qualifying early-stage startups and academic research teams get $1,000 in credits, dedicated support, and 6 months to build. Apply via the Startup Program link above.
